How are video games distributed across different genres and platforms, and how many developers are involved in creating them ?

Introduction

In 1962, the first video game, Spacewar!, was created. Since then, the development of video games has grown exponentially each year. Video games span a multitude of genres and are designed for various platforms, created by a vast number of developers. Our visualizations aim to illustrate the evolution of genres, platforms, and developers over the years from 1971 to 2023. Additionally, we will explore the relationships among these three components throughout the history of video games. Our analysis is based on the following dataset. Conclusion

The graph on the right illustrates the increasing number of video games released annually. Our analysis firstly revealed that the distribution of genres has evolved over time. Furthermore, we observed that video games are developed for a multitude of platforms, and also that the number of developers engaged in this process has grown considerably over time. Finally, we observed that the genres, platforms and developers can be linked in various ways. Thank you for your attention, we hope that our visualization has piqued your interest and that you may be more inclined to pursue further research in the video game industry.
